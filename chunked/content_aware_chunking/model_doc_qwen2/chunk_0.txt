Qwen2
Overview
Qwen2 is the new model series of large language models from the Qwen team. Previously, we released the Qwen series, including Qwen-72B, Qwen-1.8B, Qwen-VL, Qwen-Audio, etc.
Model Details
Qwen2 is a language model series including decoder language models of different model sizes. For each size, we release the base language model and the aligned chat model.
