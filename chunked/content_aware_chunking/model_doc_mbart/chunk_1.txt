mBART is one of the first methods for pretraining a complete
sequence-to-sequence model by denoising full texts in multiple languages, while previous approaches have focused only
on the encoder, decoder, or reconstructing parts of the text.
This model was contributed by valhalla. The Authors' code can be found here
Training of MBart
MBart is a multilingual encoder-decoder (sequence-to-sequence) model primarily intended for translation task.
