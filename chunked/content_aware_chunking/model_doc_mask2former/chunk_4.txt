Use [Mask2FormerImageProcessor] or [AutoImageProcessor] to prepare images and optional targets for the model.
To get the final segmentation, depending on the task, you can call [~Mask2FormerImageProcessor.post_process_semantic_segmentation] or [~Mask2FormerImageProcessor.post_process_instance_segmentation] or [~Mask2FormerImageProcessor.post_process_panoptic_segmentation].
