It randomly masks text tokens and predicts with the same information as TMLM, but it masks text blocks (areas).
BrosForTokenClassification has a simple linear layer on top of BrosModel. It predicts the label of each token.
BrosSpadeEEForTokenClassification has an initial_token_classifier and subsequent_token_classifier on top of BrosModel. initial_token_classifier is used to predict the first token of each entity, and subsequent_token_classifier is used to predict the next token of within entity.
